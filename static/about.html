About FaireFile

Protecting students from false AI cheating accusations

Our Mission

FaireFile was created to help students protect themselves from false accusations of AI cheating. As AI tools like ChatGPT become more common in education, some teachers have started embedding hidden text, invisible characters, and white text in assignment files to catch students using AI. These hidden instructions are invisible to human eyes but readable by AI systems.

When students submit these files to AI agents for help, the AI reads the hidden content and includes it in its output. Teachers then see these hidden references and accuse students of cheating, even though the students had no idea the hidden text existed. This creates an unfair situation where students are punished for content they never saw or intended to include.

FaireFile's mission is to provide transparency. We give students the tools to see exactly what hidden content exists in their assignment files before they submit them to AI agents. This empowers students to make informed decisions and protects them from false accusations.

What We Do

FaireFile is a free, web-based tool that scans PDF and DOCX assignment files for hidden content. Our scanner detects:

  • White text that blends with the background
  • Invisible Unicode characters (zero-width spaces, joiners, etc.)
  • Microscopic text with font sizes under 2pt
  • Hidden text properties in Word documents
  • Control characters and tag characters used for invisible watermarks

We show students exactly what AI agents like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Copilot will see in their files, helping them avoid false accusations.

Our Values

Transparency: We believe students have the right to know what's in their assignment files. Hidden content should not be used to trap students.

Fairness: Students should not be punished for content they cannot see. False accusations harm academic careers and mental health.

Privacy: We don't store your files. All processing happens temporarily in memory, and files are deleted immediately after analysis.

Accessibility: FaireFile is completely free with no signup required. Every student should have access to tools that protect them from false accusations.

How It Works

FaireFile uses advanced text parsing and character analysis to detect hidden content. For PDF files, we analyze characters at the lowest level, examining color values, font sizes, and Unicode character codes. For DOCX files, we check formatting properties, text colors, and hidden text flags.

Our scanner generates a risk score based on the amount and type of hidden content detected. This helps students understand the severity of hidden content in their files and make informed decisions about using AI assistance.

Contact & Support

FaireFile is committed to helping students navigate the complex landscape of AI in education. If you have questions, suggestions, or need support, please check our FAQ page or explore our blog for educational content about hidden text detection and AI in academia.

We're constantly working to improve FaireFile and add new detection capabilities. Your feedback helps us better serve the student community.
